Bio

Cameron Alexander is an artist, programmer, and scientist based in New York. His work explores the relationship between math and nature (especially in fractals, feedback, and chaotic systems), altered and exotic states of consciousness, and the essence of reality through generative art, live coded performances, and photography.

Cameron received his B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Houston in 2015. He has created generative art since 2011, and his work has been exhibited and performed nationwide.

Cameron is a member of the New York based collective LiveCode.NYC, where he livecodes visuals to acompany livecoded music at algoraves.
