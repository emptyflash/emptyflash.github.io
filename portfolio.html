Bio

Cameron Alexander is an artist, programmer, and scientist based in New York. His work focuses on exploring altered states of consciousness and the nature of reality through generative art, livecoding performances, and photography.

Cameron received his B.S. in Computer Science from the University of Houston in 2015. He has created generative art since 2011, and his work has been exhibited and performed nationwide.

Cameron is a member of the New York based collective LiveCode.NYC, where he improvises livecoded visuals to acompany livecoded visuals at algoraves.
